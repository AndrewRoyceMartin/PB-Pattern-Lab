Next Phase: Confidence That Means Something
Goal

Every pick run (CNF lane, Optimised lane, Auto lane) should clearly answer:

How much better than random is this method?

How confident are we it’s not noise?

Is it worth using? (simple decision badge)

What “Confidence” should be (simple and defensible)
Primary metric (pack-level)

Because you’re generating a 12-line card, the primary measure should be pack-based:

Primary: Δ vs Random Mean (for your chosen backtest metric)
And always show:

Percentile vs Random (e.g., “78th percentile”)

Random 5–95 band (noise range)

Permutation p-value badge (if available)

“Worth it?” badge (simple rule)

✅ Worth trying: Δ > 0 and above random P95

🟡 Promising: Δ > 0 but inside random band

⚪ No edge: Δ ≤ 0

🔴 Underperforming: clearly below random mean (optional)

This is easy to understand and honest.
