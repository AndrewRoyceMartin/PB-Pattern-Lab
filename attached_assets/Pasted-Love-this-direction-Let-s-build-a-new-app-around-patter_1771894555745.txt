New app concept
Powerball Pattern Lab (AU)

A Replit Streamlit app that:

Imports past Powerball results

Finds candidate patterns (time/date, number structure, recency, co-occurrence, etc.)

Validates them (backtests + Monte Carlo/permutation)

Generates ranked picks using only validated signals

Optionally scores “anti-popularity” (low split-risk)

Pick generation strategy design (important)
Candidate generation

Generate many candidate cards (random + weighted sampling)

Card scoring (v1 composite)

Each card gets:

1) Draw-fit score (experimental)

Based on validated patterns only:

number-level recency/frequency signals

structural fit score

carryover/pair score (if validated)

2) Anti-popularity score (practical)

Penalise likely common picks:

too many numbers ≤31

obvious sequences

repeated endings

“pretty” patterns

3) Final rank score

Weighted combination:

Balanced mode = 60% draw-fit + 40% anti-popularity

Anti-popular mode = 20% draw-fit + 80% anti-popularity

Pattern-only mode = 100% draw-fit (experimental)
